=== RBI GUIDELINES AND BANKING POLICIES ===

--- SAVINGS ACCOUNT POLICIES ---
Minimum Balance Requirements:
- Urban branches: INR 1,000 to INR 10,000 depending on bank
- Semi-urban branches: INR 500 to INR 2,000
- Rural branches: INR 250 to INR 1,000
- Basic Savings Bank Deposit (BSBD) accounts: Zero minimum balance
- Non-maintenance charges: INR 50 to INR 600 per quarter

Interest Rates on Savings Accounts:
- Regular savings accounts: 2.5% to 4% per annum
- High-balance savings accounts: Up to 7% per annum
- Interest calculated on daily closing balance
- Interest credited quarterly or monthly depending on bank

ATM Transaction Limits (RBI Guidelines):
- Minimum 5 free transactions per month at own bank ATMs
- Minimum 3 free transactions at other bank ATMs in metro cities
- Minimum 5 free transactions at other bank ATMs in non-metro cities
- Charges beyond free limit: Up to INR 21 per transaction
- Cash withdrawal limit per day: INR 10,000 to INR 1,00,000 depending on card type

--- CURRENT ACCOUNT POLICIES ---
- No interest paid on current account balances
- Overdraft facility available against current accounts
- Higher minimum balance requirements than savings accounts
- Unlimited transactions allowed
- Suitable for businesses and professionals with high transaction volumes

--- FIXED DEPOSIT POLICIES ---
Interest Rates:
- Regular FD rates: 5.5% to 7.5% per annum depending on tenure
- Senior citizen rates: Additional 0.25% to 0.75% over regular rates
- Tax-saving FD rates: Similar to regular FD rates

TDS on FD Interest:
- TDS deducted when interest exceeds INR 40,000 per year (regular)
- TDS threshold for senior citizens: INR 50,000 per year
- TDS rate with PAN: 10%
- TDS rate without PAN: 20%
- Form 15G: Submit to avoid TDS (below 60 years, income below taxable limit)
- Form 15H: Submit to avoid TDS (senior citizens)

Premature Withdrawal:
- Penalty: 0.5% to 1% reduction in applicable interest rate
- Tax-saving FDs (Section 80C): Cannot be withdrawn before 5 years
- Lock-in period for tax-saving FD: 5 years mandatory

--- RECURRING DEPOSIT POLICIES ---
- Minimum deposit: INR 100 per month in some banks
- Tenure: 6 months to 10 years
- Interest rates similar to FD rates for chosen tenure
- Missed installment penalty: Small penalty per missed installment
- Loan against RD: Available up to 80-90% of RD value
- Premature closure allowed with penalty

--- KYC (KNOW YOUR CUSTOMER) POLICIES ---
Mandatory KYC Documents:
- Identity Proof: Aadhaar card, PAN card, Passport, Voter ID, Driving License
- Address Proof: Aadhaar card, Utility bills, Rent agreement, Passport
- Photograph: Recent passport size photograph

KYC Update Requirements:
- Low risk customers: Every 10 years
- Medium risk customers: Every 8 years
- High risk customers: Every 2 years
- KYC can be updated at branch, online portal, or mobile app

Video KYC (V-KYC):
- Available for new account opening
- Requires live video interaction with bank official
- Aadhaar OTP verification required
- Valid for digital account opening

--- DIGITAL BANKING POLICIES ---
Internet Banking:
- Registration via branch or online self-registration
- Two-factor authentication mandatory
- Session timeout after inactivity
- Transaction limits set by bank and can be customized by customer

UPI (Unified Payments Interface):
- Daily transaction limit: Up to INR 1 lakh (some banks up to INR 2 lakhs)
- Per transaction limit: Up to INR 1 lakh
- Available 24x7 including bank holidays
- Linked to bank account via VPA (Virtual Payment Address)

NEFT (National Electronic Funds Transfer):
- Available 24x7
- No minimum or maximum transaction limit
- Batch-based settlement
- Free of charge as per RBI guidelines

RTGS (Real Time Gross Settlement):
- Minimum transaction amount: INR 2 lakhs
- No maximum limit
- Real-time settlement
- Available on bank working days and hours

IMPS (Immediate Payment Service):
- Available 24x7 including holidays
- Maximum limit: INR 5 lakhs per transaction
- Instant settlement
- Small charges may apply

--- DEPOSIT INSURANCE ---
DICGC Coverage:
- Each depositor insured up to INR 5 lakhs per bank
- Covers savings, fixed, recurring, and current deposits
- Both principal and interest covered within INR 5 lakhs limit
- Applies only to DICGC-registered banks
- In case of bank failure, claim processed within 90 days

--- LOCKER POLICIES ---
- Available in small, medium, and large sizes
- Annual rental varies by size and location
- Accessible only during banking hours
- Joint operation available
- Nomination facility mandatory as per RBI 2022 guidelines
- Bank liability for locker contents: Up to 100 times annual rent for losses due to bank negligence
- Bank not liable for losses due to natural calamities unless negligent

--- DORMANT AND UNCLAIMED ACCOUNTS ---
- Account becomes inoperative after 1 year of no transactions
- Account becomes dormant after 2 years of no transactions
- Unclaimed deposits transferred to RBI's DEA Fund after 10 years
- Customer can reclaim funds from bank even after transfer to DEA Fund
- Reactivation requires branch visit with KYC documents

--- FRAUD AND SECURITY POLICIES ---
Unauthorized Transaction Reporting:
- Report within 3 working days: Zero liability if bank negligent
- Report within 4-7 working days: Limited liability up to INR 10,000
- Report after 7 working days: Liability determined by bank policy
- Report to: 24x7 helpline, mobile app, net banking, or branch

OTP and Security Guidelines:
- Bank employees will NEVER ask for OTP, PIN, or password
- Do not share OTP with anyone including bank staff
- OTP valid for limited time only
- Phishing awareness: Always access banking through official channels only

Cyber Crime Reporting:
- National Cyber Crime helpline: 1930
- Online reporting: cybercrime.gov.in
- Local police FIR for significant fraud amounts

--- NOMINATION POLICIES ---
- Nomination mandatory for all deposit accounts as per RBI guidelines
- Up to 4 nominees allowed with percentage allocation
- Nominee is trustee, not necessarily legal owner
- Change nomination anytime through branch or digital channels
- Minor nominee requires guardian details
- Nominee can claim funds with death certificate and ID proof

--- GRIEVANCE REDRESSAL ---
Escalation Levels:
Level 1: Branch Manager
Level 2: Bank's Customer Care / Grievance Cell
Level 3: Nodal Officer of the Bank
Level 4: RBI Banking Ombudsman (if unresolved within 30 days)
Level 5: Consumer Court / Appellate Authority

RBI Banking Ombudsman:
- File complaint at: cms.rbi.org.in
- Free of charge
- Awards up to INR 20 lakhs for banking disputes
- Covers banks, NBFCs, and digital payment complaints
- Must approach bank first and wait 30 days before filing

=== PAYMENT AND TRANSACTION POLICIES ===

--- CHEQUE POLICIES ---
Cheque Bounce:
- Drawer charges: INR 150 to INR 750 per instance
- Legal action under Section 138 of Negotiable Instruments Act
- Criminal offense: Imprisonment up to 2 years and/or fine up to 2x cheque amount
- Cheque validity: 3 months from date of issue
- Post-dated cheques: Presented only on or after the date mentioned

Cheque Truncation System (CTS):
- Local cheques cleared within 1 working day
- Outstation cheques cleared within 1-2 working days
- Physical movement of cheques eliminated

Demand Draft (DD):
- Pre-paid instrument, cannot bounce
- Commission charged by bank
- Valid for 3 months
- Revalidation possible if expired
- Preferred for large transactions requiring guaranteed payment

--- MOBILE BANKING POLICIES ---
- Registration requires debit card or branch visit
- Biometric/MPIN authentication
- Transaction limits set per day
- Remote wipe facility if phone lost
- Registered mobile number required for OTP

=== INSURANCE POLICIES ===

--- LIFE INSURANCE ---
Term Insurance:
- Pure life cover for fixed period
- No maturity benefit if insured survives
- Low premiums
- Sum assured paid to nominee on death during term
- Tax benefit: Premium up to INR 1.5 lakhs under Section 80C

Endowment Insurance:
- Life cover plus savings component
- Maturity benefit if insured survives
- Higher premiums than term insurance
- Surrender value after 2-3 years of premium payment

ULIP (Unit Linked Insurance Plan):
- Life cover plus market-linked investment
- Lock-in period: 5 years
- Surrender charges in first 5 years
- Returns market-linked, not guaranteed
- IRDAI regulated

PMJJBY (Pradhan Mantri Jeevan Jyoti Bima Yojana):
- Eligibility: 18-50 years with savings bank account
- Premium: INR 436 per year (auto-debited)
- Coverage: INR 2 lakhs for death due to any cause
- Renewable annually

--- HEALTH INSURANCE ---
Waiting Periods:
- Initial waiting period: 30 days (no claims except accidents)
- Pre-existing disease (PED): 2-4 years
- Specific diseases (hernia, cataracts): 1-2 years
- Maternity benefit: 2-4 years

Cashless Claims:
- Available at network hospitals only
- Pre-authorization required
- Hospital settles bill directly with insurer
- TPA (Third Party Administrator) coordinates claims

Reimbursement Claims:
- Pay hospital bill upfront
- Submit claim within 30-90 days
- Original bills and discharge summary required
- Insurer reimburses approved amount

Section 80D Tax Benefits:
- Self, spouse, children: Up to INR 25,000 per year
- Senior citizen self/spouse: Up to INR 50,000
- Parents below 60: Additional INR 25,000
- Senior citizen parents: Additional INR 50,000
- Maximum total deduction: INR 1 lakh

--- MOTOR INSURANCE ---
Third-Party Insurance:
- Mandatory by law in India
- Covers damages to third party only
- Does not cover own vehicle damage
- Premium regulated by IRDAI

Comprehensive Insurance:
- Third-party coverage plus own damage
- Covers accident, fire, theft, natural disaster
- Higher premium than third-party
- Add-ons available: Zero depreciation, engine protect, NCB protect

No Claim Bonus (NCB):
- 1 year no claim: 20% discount
- 2 years: 25% discount
- 3 years: 35% discount
- 4 years: 45% discount
- 5 years: 50% discount (maximum)
- NCB belongs to policyholder, transferable to new vehicle

PMSBY (Pradhan Mantri Suraksha Bima Yojana):
- Eligibility: 18-70 years with savings bank account
- Premium: INR 20 per year
- Coverage: INR 2 lakhs accidental death/permanent total disability
- INR 1 lakh for permanent partial disability

--- INSURANCE GRIEVANCE ---
Insurance Ombudsman:
- File at: ecio.gov.in
- Free of charge
- Covers life and non-life insurance complaints
- Must approach insurer first
- Free Look Period: 15 days (offline), 30 days (online) to cancel policy
